# Remove reasoning and chat residue from deliverables

## Before

> Based on the user's prompt, I first considered three explanations. My chain of thought suggests that the sample may be too narrow, so I will now provide two recommendations. Let me know if you want me to expand this section.

## After

> The sample covers six eastern cities and does not support a national estimate. The pilot should remain limited to comparable cities until implementers report coverage, administrative cost, and appeal rates under a common protocol.

## Why

The revision removes prompt references, hidden reasoning, future-tense task narration, and the chat handoff. It retains the substantive limitation and recommendation.

## Do not copy

The geography, sample size, metrics, and recommendation are synthetic and must not be transferred to another document.
