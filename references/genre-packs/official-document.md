# Official document / 公文

## Reader and purpose

The reader is an institution, department or regulated recipient. The document records authority, action, responsibility and timing.

## Document types

通知、请示、报告、函、纪要、决定、意见、通报等。先确认文种，不混用行文目的。

## Review priorities

1. Title, addressee, issuing body, date and attachments are complete where required.
2. Background is limited to what justifies the action.
3. Requests, decisions, responsibilities and deadlines are explicit.
4. Tone follows the relationship between issuing and receiving bodies.
5. Terms, numbers, policy references and organization names are consistent.
6. Closing language matches the document type.

## Register

Official repetition and fixed expressions may be functional. Remove empty slogans and stacked praise, but do not force a casual or journalistic voice.

## Hard blocks

- changing the requested decision or authority relationship;
- inventing approvals, meetings, attachments or policy basis;
- replacing mandatory wording with vague suggestions;
- exposing drafting instructions, prompt text or internal approvals in the issued document.
