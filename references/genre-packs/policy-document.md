# Policy document / 政策文件

## Reader and purpose

The reader must understand who is governed, what is required or supported, how procedures work, and how the policy is administered.

## Required semantic checks

- issuing and responsible authority;
- legal or policy basis;
- purpose and scope;
- covered subjects and eligibility;
- rights, duties, prohibitions and exceptions;
- procedures, materials, deadlines and decision points;
- supervision, enforcement, appeal or review;
- effective date, transition and interpretation authority.

## Review priorities

1. Every obligation has a clear subject and action.
2. “应、须、可以、不得、原则上”等 modal terms keep their exact force.
3. Defined terms are used consistently.
4. Cross-references, numbering and effective dates are correct.
5. Public explanation is separated from normative clauses.
6. Promotional language does not replace operational provisions.

## Editing limits

Do not naturalize away necessary repetition, parallel clause structure or official terminology. If legal validity or policy authority is uncertain, mark it for professional review rather than silently revising it.

## Hard blocks

- changing rights, duties, eligibility, deadlines, discretion or exceptions;
- inventing legal authority;
- removing defined terms for stylistic variety;
- leaving drafting notes, prompt text or model reasoning in the promulgated text.
