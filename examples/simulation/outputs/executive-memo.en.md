# Decision memo: temporary support staffing

## Decision requested

Approve the reassignment of 4 employees from onboarding to customer support for 14 days. The VP of Operations would own the reassignment and review progress on July 15, 2026.

## Evidence and options

As of June 30, 2026, the support queue contained 1,240 tickets; 46% were more than 7 days old, and the staffing plan was 6 FTE below target. The available options are to freeze low-priority intake for 2 weeks, reassign 4 onboarding employees for 14 days, or outsource 300 tickets. The supplied material does not include comparable cost or throughput estimates for the three options.

## Recommendation and risk

The temporary reassignment is recommended because it adds internal capacity without suspending intake. The reported trade-off is an 18% increase in onboarding response time during the reassignment. The July 15 checkpoint should review the support backlog and onboarding response time before the reassignment is extended or ended.
